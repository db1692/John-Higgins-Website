Speaking

I provide a series of set-piece workshops and talks of varying length around: Speaking up about speaking up and listening up Speaking up about taken for granted and stuck patterns of thinking at work and in society These set pieces can also be supplemented by recorded and/or live conversations with others

Researching

I carry out participative research into individual, group and organisational experiences of power, voice and silence The research is carried out in such a way as to foreground those experiences and people who are frequently over-looked – the opposite of the ‘view from the top’ that dominates organisational and managerial studies

Editing & Authoring

I work with people who are trying to find their voice in written form. The precise nature of the editorial services varies enormously from shaping an idea through to ghost writing

Advising

I take part in short and long-term conversations with people who are seeking to make sense of their life – be that in terms of a very specific issue they are looking to address at work or a more existential inquiry into what role people want work to play in their lives

Fees

Fees vary according to the nature of the work, organization, presenting issue and quality of personal connection
